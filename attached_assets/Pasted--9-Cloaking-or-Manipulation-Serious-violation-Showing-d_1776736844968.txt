🧠 9. Cloaking or Manipulation

    •    Redirecting users unexpectedly
    •    Keyword stuffing

🧾 1. Business Legitimacy (Your biggest blocker)
🔴 Current state (high risk)
Your site does not clearly establish a verifiable business entity.
A reviewer will ask:
    •    Who owns this company?
    •    Where is it located?
    •    Can a customer hold it accountable?
Right now, those answers are weak or missing.
✅ What you need to add (non-negotiable)
Create a “Trust Layer” section across your site:
Footer (visible on every page):
    •    Registered business name
    •    Full address (not just city)
    •    Phone number (working)
    •    Business email (domain-based, not Gmail)
New page: “About Us” must include:
    •    Founder or company story
    •    Why this business exists
    •    Real photos (office, team, inventory)
    •    GST / registration (if in India—this is a HUGE trust booster)
👉 Without this, Bing often treats you as a lead-gen scam risk

🔐 2. Legal & Policy Compliance (currently insufficient)
🔴 Likely issues
Most rental sites fail here because policies are:
    •    too short
    •    copied templates
    •    missing key clauses
✅ You must have detailed, real policies:
Privacy Policy
    •    What data you collect
    •    Cookies + tracking
    •    Third-party sharing
Terms & Conditions
    •    Rental eligibility
    •    ID verification requirements
    •    Usage restrictions
Rental Agreement (critical for your niche)
    •    Deposit rules
    •    Damage liability
    •    Late return penalties
    •    Replacement cost terms
Refund & Cancellation Policy
    •    Exact refund timelines
    •    Conditions for denial
👉 These must be specific, not generic—Bing reviewers can tell instantly.

💰 3. Financial Transparency (major trust signal)
🔴 Current risk
Rental businesses are flagged if:
    •    Pricing is unclear
    •    Hidden fees exist
    •    Deposit logic is vague
✅ Fix this with clarity:
On each product page, explicitly show:
    •    Rental price (per day/week/month)
    •    Security deposit (exact amount)
    •    Delivery charges (if any)
    •    What happens if:
    ◦    device is damaged
    ◦    device is lost
    ◦    late return occurs
👉 Add a “Pricing Explained” section—this alone can improve approval odds.

⚠️ 4. Claims & Messaging (high rejection risk)
🔴 Risky phrases to audit/remove:
    •    “100% safe”
    •    “guaranteed”
    •    “best” / “#1”
    •    “same-day delivery” (without proof)
✅ Replace with:
    •    “Available in select locations”
    •    “Subject to availability”
    •    “Verified devices with quality checks”
👉 Bing flags unverifiable claims very aggressively
